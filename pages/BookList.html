Books and Podcasts

Title: Deep Work
Author: Cal Newport
My thoughts: This book revolutionized the way I organize my time and work efforts. The author argues in Part I that deep work, work that is both focused and valuable, is necessary to success and satsifaction in all parts of life. Part II is then filled with strategies to drain the shallow work from your life and focus on the deep, meaningful work. Overall this book was a great read and provides great examples of model innovators, leaders, and entreprenuers.
